Hack@BVP 3.0

Hackathon: Hack@BVP 3.0 is a 12-hour hackathon. Its mission is to provide a thrilling experience to students, developers, and anyone interesting in coding. Teams of developers, designers, and marketers will come together to build functioning apps, websites, programs or anything that tackle environmental issues in just 12 hours. Our theme is based on Issues like fit India, Anti plastic , tourism , population and traffic control. These topics are gaining a lot of much- needed attention and prompting discussions. At a local level, communities are addressing the issues of Plastic ban and population control, but we as students should contribute more towards it so let’s do something that makes difference in the society

Hack, Invent and Create.

A 12 hrs software development competetion. Shoutout to all computer nerds and gizmo geeks.

Hack@BVP 3.0 is a chance for all developers, hobbyists, students, or anyone else to build an innovative application revolving around the theme (which will be declared soon) in a full day fueled by creativity, passion and grit. Judging Criteria:


Theme:

How much the solution is aligned with mentioned theme. Uniqueness/ Originality Whether the idea has been thought already by someone.

Uniqueness/ Originality:

Whether the idea has been thought already by someone.

Feasability:

The functional and technical feasibility of the solution.

Cost of Implementation:

How much cost would it need(lower the cost you need better the solution).

Effectiveness of Idea:

How much it would effect the world.

Implementation completeness:

How much of it you have successfully implemented and demo




Rules:
