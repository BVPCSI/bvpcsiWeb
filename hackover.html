About HacKOver

HackOver is 24-hour hackathon organized by BVPCSI. It’s mission is to provide a thrilling experience to students, developers, and anyone interested in coding. Teams of developers, designers and marketers will come together to build functioning apps, websites, programs or anything that tackle any kind of issue related to our daily lives in just 24 hours.

This is an open theme, like big data, health, IOT, sociall and environment related issues, disaster management etc, based hackathon with no limit on technology stack. Although it is an open theme hackathon preference will be given to the team focusing on social and environmental topics as these are the topics gaining a lot of much needed attention and prompting discussions of disaster relief and preparedness.

Cash prize worth INR 10k to the winners, Cash prize worth INR 5K to the first runner up. Goodies/swags for all participants.
