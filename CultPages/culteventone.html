Juvenile Care Event

Volunteers of BVPCSI collaborated with Juvenile Care, an NGO which operates to aid underprivileged children, to interact with these children in the slums of Tagore Garden. The Society of Social Services (SSS), a sub-chapter of BVPCSI, arranged it on 26th January 2020 to celebrate Republic Day in a way which gives back to the country.
The volunteers taught the children rhymes and patriotic songs and sung them with fervour, supplemented with a little origami. The day had a successful outcome.
