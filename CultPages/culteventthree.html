Blind School Visit

Society of Social Services (SSS), a sub-chapter of BVPCSI, organized a visit to the ‘Akhil Bhartiya Netrahin Sangh’ on 22nd February. The NGO runs a school for blind children in Tagore Garden. Around 20 members interacted with the children. They took quizzes, asked riddles, played guessing games and had fun with tongue twisters. The winners were also rewarded with small gifts.
