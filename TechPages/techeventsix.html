Adobe Photoshop Workshop

Photoshop is all about showing your creativity skills whether to do some editing in a wedding album or to replace one's face with another and much more

The Creativity team at BVPCSI organizes several sessions on Adobe Photoshop for the student members to tickle the Right Brain

Photoshop, one of the basic tools for the modern man helps to make gorgeous digital portraits and some of the best and dankest memes.

We are no stranger to the boon and bliss of Adobe Photoshop, over the years Photoshop has brought us some of the most controversial moments to the funniest. Photoshop been the industry standard image manipulation program for so long that its name has become a verb: It is common parlance to say that an image has been “photo shopped” or even just “shopped."

Shopped, in this context, is synonymous with edited, manipulated or faked -- often regardless of the software actually used.

BVPCSI in association with the Creativity team organizes sessions on Photoshop for the student members to truly bring out their creative sides. Art is considered as one of the best ways to bust stress and keep mind sharp.
